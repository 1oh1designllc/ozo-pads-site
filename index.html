Features
Everything a coach needs.
Nothing they don't.
OZO PADS is built for the coach holding the mitts — not the fighter training alone. Every feature is designed around how pad work actually happens.
◈
COMBINATION LIBRARY
Build your full combo library using standard boxing notation. Jabs, crosses, hooks, body shots, kicks, knees, elbows. Southpaw mirroring flips the sequence automatically.
▶
HANDS-FREE SESSIONS
The session runner calls every action out loud so your hands stay on the mitts. Auto-advance steps through combinations at your chosen pace.
◉
FIGHTER ROSTER
Every fighter gets a full profile — stance, weight class, skill level, fight record, and coaching notes. Build combination libraries specific to each fighter's game plan.
◧
SESSION PLAN TEMPLATES
Ten pre-built session plans across all four sport modes. Start from a template and customise, or build from scratch.
◭
FIGHTER PROGRESS
Weekly and monthly session charts, duration trends, and most-drilled combinations for every fighter on your roster.
✦
AI COMBO VARIATIONS
Stuck in a rut? OZO PADS Pro generates AI-powered variations of any combination — new angles, different rhythms, same fundamental structure.
◫
TRAINING LOG
Log every session with coaching notes while details are fresh. Full history for every fighter, searchable across your entire library.
⌚
WATCH COMPANION
Large-text glanceable view for pad work. Full session runner on your wrist — auto-advance, rest countdown, rep tracking.
